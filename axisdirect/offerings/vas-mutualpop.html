AxisDirect Mutual Fund Suggestions have a huge variety in terms of the suggested investment horizon and the asset classes in which these funds further invest the money of the investors.

Investment Horizon Money invested in asset classes
3 Years to 5 years Major portion of the funds is invested in Equity and depending on the scheme objective some portion may or may not be invested in fixed income securities.
6 months to 18 months Most of the schemes falling in this suggested investment horizon range invest equal portions of the funds in Equity and Fixed Income instruments. Again, the exact allocation depends in the scheme objective.
1 day to 12 months These schemes generally invest the funds in Fixed Income instruments like Government Securities and Bonds. A very little or no portion of the funds is invested in Equity depending on Scheme Objective.
