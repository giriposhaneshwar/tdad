Futures

Allows you to hedge your positions or take leveraged bets on stocks & indices

  • Future Margin

    Trade in Futures & option on selected stocks & Indices with lesser capital as compared to cash

  • Intraday Features

    Allows you the flexibility of taking intraday positions in futures at 50% lesser margin as compared to normal futures.

  • Future - Cover Order

    Allows you higher leverage than intraday orders by ensuring that you place a stop loss orders against your futures position.

Options

A financial derivative contract that allows you right to buy or sell stock or indices at predetermined price on future date

  • Options - Margin

    Hedge your positions or take a bet on future price of a stock by buying or selling options with our Margin orders

  • Options – Cover Order

    Allows you higher leverage than normal options orders by ensuring that you place a stop loss orders against your options position.
