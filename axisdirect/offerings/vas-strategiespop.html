Trading Ideas that recommend positions in two or more Derivative Contracts

Strategy Name Description Investment Horizon
Pair Trade When unusual price difference is identified in stocks which usually move together, opposite positions are recommended in these stocks Upto 1 month
Hedged Futures These are generally Buy future + Buy Put or Sell Future + Buy Call combinations recommended in a stock or Index. Upto 1 month
Spread strategies Two or more positions in call and /or put options are recommended with limited risk and limited Profit. Upto 1 month
Speculative Option strategies Two or more positions in call and /or put options are recommended which may have limited or unlimited Risk and Profit depending on the combination recommended. Upto 1 month
