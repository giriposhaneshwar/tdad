These are the Trading Ideas wherein positions in two or more Derivative Contracts are recommended together. Investment horizon is limited till the Expiry of the Contract. These are recommended based on market condtions.

Strategy Name Description
Pair Trade Two stocks/Indices or a pair of one stock and Index are identified which usually show coorelation in their price movement. Whenever an unusual price difference is identified between the two in such identified pairs, a pair trade is recommended assuming that the unsual difference will be corrected. Relatively underpriced contract is recommended to buy and the other is recommended to sell.
Hedged Futures These are generally Buy future + Buy Put or Sell Future + Buy Call combinations recommended in a stock or Index. Position in future is expected to earn profit and call/put act as hedging instruments in case of adverse price movement of stock/Index.
Spread strategies Two or more positions in call and /or put options are recommended in Spread Strategies. These strategies have limited risk and limited Profit.
Speculative Option strategies Two or more positions in call and /or put options are recommended in Speculative Option Strategies. The Profit/loss in expected in these strategies may be limited or unlimited. It depends on the combination recommended.
