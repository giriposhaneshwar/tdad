Mr Rajiv Anand is Group Executive – Retail Banking at Axis Bank and handles retail liabilities, consumer lending & payments, electronic banking, marketing, regional distribution, investment products, rural lending and financial inclusion.

Mr. Anand joined the Bank from its asset management arm, Axis Asset Management Co. Ltd., in 2013, where he held the position of Managing Director & CEO. Over the last 22 years of his career, Mr. Anand has focused on the financial services industry, having held key management positions at leading global financial institutions. He brings strengths in capital markets and building new businesses to his responsibilities at the Bank
